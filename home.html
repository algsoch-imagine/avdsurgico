Our Product Range

At AVD Surgico, we offer a wide variety of surgical instruments designed to meet the needs of medical professionals. Our product range includes but is not limited to:

  • Surgical Scissors: Available in various sizes and types, including straight, curved, blunt, and sharp, designed for precision cutting.
  • Forceps: A diverse range of forceps for grasping, holding, and manipulating tissues and materials.
  • Clamps: High-quality clamps for controlling blood flow and securing tissues during surgical procedures.
  • Retractors: Essential tools for holding back tissues and organs, providing better visibility and access during surgery.
  • Needle Holders: Designed for holding needles securely during suturing.
  • Speculums: Used for opening orifices or cavities for examination.
